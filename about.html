Get To Know Samantha

About Me

I’m Samantha Reily, an experienced Accessibility Analyst dedicated to helping organizations make their digital spaces inclusive and accessible to everyone. With a background in ensuring compliance with ADA and WCAG standards, I have a proven track record of conducting accessibility audits, offering practical solutions, and helping businesses enhance their user experience for all individuals, regardless of ability. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Human Computer Interaction from Arizona State University. Additionally, I am a Certified Professional in Accessibility Core Competencies (CPACC), issued by the International Association of Accessibility Professionals (IAAP) in November 2020. I am also certified as a DHS Trusted Tester, accredited by the U.S. Department of Homeland Security.

I’m passionate about accessibility because it’s not just a legal requirement—it’s about creating better, more inclusive digital experiences for everyone.

Accessibility – Connecting People and Technology

Accessibility is not just about compliance; it's about people. It's about ensuring that everyone, regardless of their abilities, can engage with your digital content.

My role as an Accessibility Analyst is to guide businesses in making their websites, apps, and digital platforms user-friendly and compliant with legal standards like the ADA and WCAG. With proper accessibility measures, we can break down barriers and create a seamless digital experience for all.

Accessibility is a continuous journey. As technology evolves, so should our efforts to make it accessible. Let's work together to create a world where digital spaces are open and welcoming to all.

Bringing Accessibility to Your Digital Presence

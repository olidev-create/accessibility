Accessibility – Connecting People and Technology

Accessibility is not just about compliance; it's about people. It's about ensuring that everyone, regardless of their abilities, can engage with your digital content.

My role as an Accessibility Analyst is to guide businesses in making their websites, apps, and digital platforms user-friendly and compliant with legal standards like the ADA and WCAG. With proper accessibility measures, we can break down barriers and create a seamless digital experience for all.

Accessibility is a continuous journey. As technology evolves, so should our efforts to make it accessible. Let's work together to create a world where digital spaces are open and welcoming to all.
